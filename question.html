解：$\because$$AB=DC$，$AD=BC$，$BD=DB$ $\therefore \triangle B C D \cong \triangle D A B$ $\therefore \angle C B D=\angle A D B=30^{\circ}$ $\because A B=C D, B F=D E$ $\therefore \triangle B C F \cong \triangle D A E$ $\therefore \angle B C F=\angle D A E$ $\because \angle A E B=120^{\circ}$ $\therefore \angle A E D=60^{\circ}$ $\because \angle A D B=30^{\circ}$ $\therefore \angle D A E=90^{\circ}$ $\therefore \angle B C F=90^{\circ}$ 故填$90$．
